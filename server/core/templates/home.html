Welcome! My name is Bethany, I'm a fully licensed clinician with advanced training from "Univertity" and a few ongoing professional developments. My speciality is based on evident modalities such as EMDR, DBT, CCPT, and IFS to guide children, adults, and families toward healing and resilience. I build each session specifically to you, whether you need support for anxiety, trauma, or even want resources for educators and service providers, I offer a compassionate and effective pathway based on results from evidences to acquire genuine transformation.
